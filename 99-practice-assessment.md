# Practice Assessment - AI-300

> **AI-300 is currently in beta.** Microsoft has **not yet released an official free practice assessment** for this exam. Use the alternatives below.

## Best free practice options

1. **[AI Copilot Quiz](17-copilot-quiz.md)** - clipboard-prompt + open Microsoft Copilot / Gemini / ChatGPT / Claude. Recommended primary drill tool while no Microsoft assessment exists.
2. **AI-102 + DP-100 official assessments** - AI-300 reuses concepts from both.
   - [AI-102 practice assessment](https://learn.microsoft.com/credentials/certifications/azure-ai-engineer/)
   - [DP-100 practice assessment](https://learn.microsoft.com/credentials/certifications/azure-ai-engineer/)
3. **Microsoft Learn modules** - every module ends with a knowledge check. Pace yourself through the [AI-300 study path](11-microsoft-resources.md).

## Day-of-exam reminders

- **Beta exam:** results take up to **2 weeks** after the beta period closes. Don't panic about no instant score.
- 100 minutes, ~50 questions, includes case studies / drag-drop.
- Mark questions for review; revisit if time allows.
- Lab section possible - read the full scenario first.
- Watch for "no infrastructure to manage", "predictable peak latency", "without storing credentials" - magic-words map straight to PTU / OIDC / serverless answers.

## Final-day tactic
- Skim [05-exam-cheatsheet.md](05-exam-cheatsheet.md) at breakfast.
- Run 10 questions of [Rapid-Fire mode](17-copilot-quiz.md) on your phone before leaving.
- Bring photo ID. Test your camera + mic 30 minutes early if remote.
